Account Deletion – EarnBook

If you want to delete your EarnBook account, please follow the steps below carefully.

  1. Send an email to support@earnbook.in
  2. Use your registered mobile number or email ID
  3. Mention subject: Request for Account Deletion

After successful verification, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 working days.

Data that will be deleted

  • Personal profile details
  • Transaction history
  • Wallet data

Data that may be retained

  • Legal or regulatory required records (if any)
This account deletion policy is applicable to all EarnBook users and complies with Google Play’s User Data & Account Deletion guidelines.
For any queries, contact support@earnbook.in

Account Deletion – EarnBook

If you want to delete your EarnBook account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@earnbook.in
  2. Use your registered mobile number / email ID
  3. Subject: Request for Account Deletion

Your account will be deleted within 7 working days.

Data that will be deleted

  • Personal profile details
  • Transaction history
  • Wallet data

Data that may be retained

  • Legal or regulatory required records (if any)

For queries: support@earnbook.in
